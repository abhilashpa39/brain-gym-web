What is Brain Gym?

Brain Gym is a gamified Android app designed to boost your memory, focus, logic, and cognitive speed. Daily challenges, XP rewards, and personal progress tracking keep your mind sharp and engaged.

Features

🎨 Color Challenge

Boost visual memory with pattern recognition games.

🧩 Memory Grid

Train short-term memory and logic through timed puzzles.

📈 Daily Progress

Track your streaks, XP, and task completions visually.
