Privacy Policy

Brain Gym is committed to respecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle data for our website visitors and app users.

📌 What We Collect

We do not collect any personal data from users on this website or through our app. This includes names, emails, age, and location.

🔍 Analytics

We do not use any analytics tools on this website. Our Android app may use tools like Firebase for crash reporting and performance tracking, but no personal identifiers are collected or stored.

📢 Ads and Tracking

We do not show any ads or use trackers on our website. AdMob is used in the Android app only.

🤝 Third-party Sharing

We do not share any user data with third parties.

🍪 Cookies and Local Storage

This website does not use cookies or any form of local storage to track or store user information.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at connect@braingym.blog.
